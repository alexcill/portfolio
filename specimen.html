GT Sectra Specimen

Category
tipography & ux – ui project

Years
2023-2024

This project features a redesign of the GT Sectra specimen, designed by Dominik Huber, Marc Kappeler, and Noël Leu for Grilli Type foundry. The project analyzes the font - family and styles, history, and uses.

This project shows the GT Sectra font, its sub-families – GT Sectra Display and GT Sectra Fine – and their 30 styles. It begins with the font’s history, presented with micro animations to reveal its evolution step by step. The sections designed for this project include: typeface information, samples, glyphs, in use, and finally a section dedicated to hypothetical merchandise.
