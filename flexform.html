Flexform Studio

Category
ux – ui project

Years
2023-2024

Project group
Alessia Russo

Flexform Studio is a project that involves the redesign of a section of the original website of Flexform, in which the aim is to use AI technology to furnish virtual spaces with the products of this brand.


The project offers a vision of how the customer would configure with the contribution of AI, offering personalized filters based on users’ behavior.

Flexform Studio is the new custom configurator service designed to meet the needs of three different target audiences, studied and analyzed – luxury customers, hotel entrepreneurs, and design professionals; it can be accessed through the nav bar or directly from a product detail page (pdp). For this new service, AI creates different spaces, offering users various options through prompts, which become increasingly tailored. At the end of the customization, the user will also be able to save their works.
