Unmelt

Category
ux – ui project

Years
2023-2024

Project group
Joane Barrenetxea
David Pedroza

Unmelt is a project that involves the development of a visual identity and all its hypothetical applications; especially the website, in which the brand’s identity and mission are outlined. Unmelt is a product line initiative that incentivizes a more sustainable way of consuming; a mission to rewind on global warming and prevent the melting of ice and the sea level rise.


The logo can be used in five different ways. It has four levels; the more levels are full, the more the product helps the environment. If none of the levels are, it means the product contributes with at least 80% of materials coming from organic or recycled sources. As the number of full levels increases, the contribution goes up.

The website has been created with the intention of fully conveying the brand’s values. Within the prototype, you can find several pages dedicated to partners, collaboration products, as well as original unmelt items; along with a space dedicated to users and/or future collaborations. The shape of the logo has been emphasized and used internally to maintain visual coherence throughout the site. The use of seemingly low-resolution images also aims to conserve energy during navigation.
