Ray-Ban Remix

Category
editorial & ux – ui project

Years
2023-2024

Project group
Ilaria Gasparo

For this project, a landing page has been designed to celebrate the 10 years of Ray-Ban Remix: the customization service through which users will be able to obtain a pair of completely personalized glasses.

For the development of the landing page, we decided to play with the concept of remix: it’s immediately noticeable in the hero video, which focuses on a continuous change of glasses and colors. Another focal point of the project is the use of bold typography that accompanies us throughout all sections of the prototype.
