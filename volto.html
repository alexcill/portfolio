“Volto” is an editorial project; a magazine aimed at drawing attention to those who often go unnoticed, those who suffer injustices and remain silent about them. This project brings with it many untold stories to which we have tried to give a voice

The website maintains the style of the printed magazine; inside, there will be articles, both with corrections or without, letters, and interviews. To stay in touch with users, a section has been created where they can write to Volto.
